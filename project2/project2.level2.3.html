a spider

lives in

the web

of Wyrd,

weaving its


threads as


he wills.



he lays


down tripwires


for unwary


feet, and


laughs when


we fall



on our



faces.


he gives





strife to





the kindred




in the




mead-hall




of life,




building a




fire for




our hands,



but if





we put






our hands






in is






it our






fault or






his? we








knew it








was hot








'fore we








touched it.
